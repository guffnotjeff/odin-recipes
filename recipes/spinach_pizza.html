Greek Pizza with Spinach, Feta and Olives

Description

A deliciously unique twist on a classic, spinach pizza features a crispy crust topped with a savory blend of melted cheeses, fresh spinach, and a hint of garlic. Perfectly seasoned, this pizza combines the wholesome goodness of spinach with the rich flavors of traditional pizza, making it a satisfying choice for vegetarians and pizza lovers alike.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese, divided
  • 12 inch pre-baked Italian pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, coarsely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon oil from the sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup pitted kalamata olives, coarsely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 cups baby spinach leaves
  • 1/2 small red onion, halved and thinly sliced

    • Steps

    1. Adjust oven rack to lowest position, and heat oven to 450 degrees.
    2. Mix mayonnaise, garlic and 1/2 cup feta in a small bowl.
    3. Place pizza crust on a cookie sheet; spread mayonnaise mixture over pizza, then top with tomatoes, olives and oregano.
    4. Bake until heated through and crips, about 10 minutes.
    5. Toss spinach and onion with the 1 tablespoon sun-dried tomato oil.
    6. Place the spinach mixture and the remaining 1/2 cup feta cheese on top.
    7. Return to oven and bake until cheese melts, bout 2 minutes.
    8. Cut into 6 slices and serve.
